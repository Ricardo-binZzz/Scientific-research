# Paper Summary

- Title: Adaptive clamping fixture design
- Authors: Zhang; Li
- Source: Journal of Manufacturing Systems
- Year: 2024
- DOI: 10.1000/demo-fixture

## Problem

Traditional fixtures have limited adaptability for complex parts.

## Method

The paper builds an adaptive clamping mechanism and verifies stress distribution with finite element analysis.

## Data

Clamping force, displacement, and stress response.

## Key Figures

Figure 3 mechanism layout; Figure 6 stress cloud map.

## Main Result

The adaptive fixture reduces deformation under the same clamping load.

## Limitation

The validation sample is small and only covers one fixture layout.

## Reuse Value

Useful as a comparison baseline for fixture stiffness and clamping-force evaluation.
